Spacelab.

We are the Security, Privacy, and Cryptographic Engineering Lab, a research group at the City College of New York. The members of the Spacelab are involved in the research and teaching of systems, network, and embedded security, privacy and censorship resistance, practical cryptography, and other related areas. The Spacelab is captained by Prof. Tushar Jois.

Interested in joining the Spacelab? Take a look at this page!
